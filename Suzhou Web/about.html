Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST) is a private, multi-disciplinary research university located at Avenida Wai Long, Taipa, Macao. It is ranked #464 in the QS Global World Rankings and is recognized for its very high research output.
The university's mission is to promote cultural exchange, nurture intellectual growth, foster economic development, and further societal progress, aligning with the needs of Macau and the rapid development of the region.
